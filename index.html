About Me

“The only thing that is stopping you from where you are to where you want to go is your comfort zone”

Dhaval Gaudier

Hello! My Name is Azucena Gonzalez. I am a Full-Stack Developer with a Certificate in Web Development from the University of Arizona with experience developing 6 full-stack web applications from mockup to deployment. I have experience utilizing debugging Algorithms and data structures, as well as DevOps agile methodologies and processes.

I am organized and a self-motivated problem-solver with a desire to grow and a passion for collaboration. I was selected by management in previous roles to mentor peers on Business Process Improvement opportunities at Edward Jones, we worked to identify inefficiencies, we achieved to consolidate and digitize the portfolio transfer process and department archives and data/resource storage. I am currently seeking to leverage skills in JavaScript, Node.js, and SQL/MongoDB with an organization that promotes growth and embraces new technology.

During my free time, I like to travel, hike, and volunteer. I took a year off from work and went to Ghana in West Africa to volunteer as Director of Operations for a nonprofit school. Having first hand experienced the digital divide, I came back home more appreciative and inspired to learn more about the tech industry. Intrigued by the complexity of a constantly changing industry, I joined the University of Arizona Full Stack Development program and have been learning and challenging myself every day.
