About Me

“The only thing that is stopping you from where you are to where you want to go is your comfort zone”

Dhaval Gaudier

Hi there! My Name is Azucena Gonzalez. A former business professional that turned into a Full Stack Software Engineer. What I love about programming is that you never stop learning. More importantly, I love the ability to create anything imagined, test it and improve it as technology progresses. It is challenging, engaging and highly rewarding. At times it can be hair splitting yet in the end it is fulfilling and worth every bit of effort.

During my free time, I love to travel, hike, work on personal coding projects and occassionaly seek adventures. I took a year off from work and went to Ghana in West Africa to volunteer as Director of Operations for a nonprofit school.

Having first hand experienced the digital divide, I came back home more appreciative and inspired to learn more about the tech industry. Intrigued by the complexity of a constantly changing industry, I joined the University of Arizona Full Stack Development program and have been learning and challenging myself every day.
